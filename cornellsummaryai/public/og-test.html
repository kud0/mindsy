💡 Tips for Better Social Sharing

  • Image Size: Optimal size is 1200x630px for best display
  • File Size: Keep image under 8MB (preferably under 1MB)
  • Description: Keep under 300 characters for best display
  • Title: Keep under 60 characters for best display
  • Cache: Social platforms cache images, use debugger tools to refresh
